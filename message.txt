India, a vast and diverse South Asian nation, is the world's largest democracy by population. Bordered by the Himalayas to the north and surrounded by the Indian Ocean on three sides, its geography spans everything from snow-capped peaks to tropical coastlines and deserts. The country is the birthplace of major religions including Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism .
Historically, India was home to the ancient Indus Valley Civilization and has been a significant trade route and colonial territory. It gained independence from British rule in 1947, a movement largely shaped by Mahatma Gandhi's non-violent resistance.
