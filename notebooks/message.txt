India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by land area, the second-most populous country, and the most populous democracy in the world. 

India shares borders with Pakistan to the west; China, Nepal, and Bhutan to the north; and Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east. It is bounded by the Indian Ocean on the south, the Arabian Sea on the southwest, and the Bay of Bengal on the southeast.

India has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its diversity in languages, religions, and traditions. Major festivals celebrated in India include Diwali, Holi, Eid, Christmas, and many others, reflecting the country's pluralism. 

The Indian political system is a parliamentary system, and it is divided into 28 states and 8 Union territories. The capital of India is New Delhi, and the largest city is Mumbai.

India has made significant contributions to the world in fields such as science, mathematics, art, and technology. It is also known for its cuisine, which varies significantly across different regions of the country.
